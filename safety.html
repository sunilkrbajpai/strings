Dating Safety Guide

Meeting new people is exciting, but we want you to be safe. Read these tips before you start chatting or meeting up.

Online Safety

  • Never send money: Never send money or financial information, no matter how sad or urgent the story sounds.
  • Keep conversations on Strings: Bad actors often try to move the chat to SMS or WhatsApp immediately. Stay on the app where our filters can protect you.
  • Protect personal info: Don't share your home address, work address, or daily routine details until you really know someone.

Meeting In Person

  • Meet in public: Always meet for the first time in a populated, public place‚Äînever at a private home or secluded area.
  • Tell a friend: Inform a friend or family member of your plans, including who you are meeting, where, and when.
  • Stay in control: Be in charge of your own transportation. Don't rely on your date to pick you up or drop you off.
