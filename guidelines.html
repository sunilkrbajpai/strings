Community Guidelines

Strings is a place for making genuine connections. To ensure that everyone has a good time, we've established these guidelines. If you cross the line, you may be banned.

Nudity & Sexual Content

Strings is not a place for nudity or sexually explicit content. Don't post photos that display genitals or nudity. Profiles or photos that are sexually explicit or pornographic will be removed.

Harassment

Do not engage in targeted abuse or harassment against any user. This includes unsolicited sexual content, stalking, or bullying. If someone unmatches you or isn't interested, leave them alone.

Violence & Physical Harm

We have a zero-tolerance policy for violent content. Violent descriptions, photos involving weapons, or threats of physical harm to yourself or others are strictly prohibited.

Hate Speech

Any content that promotes, advocates for, or condones racism, bigotry, hatred, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religious belief, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, or disability is not allowed.

Impersonation & Authenticity

Be yourself. Don't pretend to be someone else. Do not use photos of celebrities or other people. Parody accounts are not allowed.

Private Information

Don't publicly broadcast your own private information or anyone else's. This includes social security numbers, passports, passwords, or financial information.
